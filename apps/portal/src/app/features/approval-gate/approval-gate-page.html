Approval Gate

Approval Gate evaluates and records approval eligibility. It never merges or deploys code.

Collects trusted evidence from Review, Testing, Patch, Git, Pull Request, CI, and Repair operations; evaluates organization policies; records automated and human governance decisions. Does not merge, GitHub-approve, push, modify patches, or trigger CI.
